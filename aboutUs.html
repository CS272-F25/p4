About CineRewind

Bringing back the magic of video rental stores

Our Mission

At CineRewind, we believe that the experience of browsing through a video rental store should never be forgotten. Our mission is to bring back the nostalgia and excitement of discovering new films in a retro-themed digital environment. We combine the charm of the 1980s and 1990s video rental era with modern technology to create an unforgettable movie rental experience.

Our Story

How CineRewind Began

CineRewind was founded in 2024 by a group of movie enthusiasts who grew up during the golden age of video rental stores. We remember the excitement of walking through aisles of VHS tapes and DVDs, reading the back covers, and discovering hidden gems.

With the rise of streaming services, we felt something was missing—the personal touch, the community aspect, and the thrill of physical browsing. CineRewind was born from the desire to preserve that experience while adapting it for the digital age.

Today, we're proud to offer a curated selection of movies with a retro-themed interface that brings back those nostalgic feelings while providing the convenience of modern online rental.
